// Our Edge

Three Pillars That
Differentiate Us

01 ——
Data-Driven Sourcing

Most brokers operate on relationships alone. We layer analytics on top — tracking allocation cycles, pricing volatility, and lead-time patterns across global markets to time our sourcing decisions with precision. You get better pricing because we buy smarter.

02 ——
BIS Compliance First

Every transaction involving export-controlled components is reviewed against the U.S. Bureau of Industry and Security's Entity List and EAR regulations. We will not move hardware that cannot be fully documented — no exceptions, no workarounds.

03 ——
End-to-End Logistics

From North American pickup through final delivery in Asia, the UAE, or Europe — we manage the full freight chain. Our partnerships with established cross-border freight operators like UUCargo ensure reliable, trackable, compliant international shipments.

// Trade Corridors

Where We
Operate

MICA Supply maintains active trade corridors across four regions, sourcing where supply is available and delivering where demand exists. Our core origination markets are North America and Europe; our primary delivery destinations are Hong Kong, mainland Asia, and the UAE.


All cross-border shipments are accompanied by full commercial invoices, packing lists, and — where required — End-User Statements and export license documentation.
