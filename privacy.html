Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

MICA Supply LTD. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our B2B clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit micasupply.ca or submit a Request for Quote (RFQ).

1. Information We Collect

  • Business Information: When you submit an RFQ, we collect your corporate email address, component requirements (BOM/Part Numbers), and target pricing.
  • Compliance Data: For regulated hardware, we collect End-User Statements (EUS) detailing your corporate entity, physical location, and intended end-use application.
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect aggregated, non-personally identifiable information such as browser type, geographic region (e.g., country/city level), and page navigation patterns to optimize our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for B2B trade purposes, including:

  • Processing your RFQs and sourcing hardware across our network.
  • Conducting mandatory Export Administration Regulations (EAR) and Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) compliance screenings.
  • Coordinating cross-border logistics and freight forwarding.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your data. Information is only shared with trusted third parties to fulfill your business requests:

  • Logistics Partners: Freight details may be shared with carriers (e.g., UUCargo) for customs clearance and delivery.
  • Regulatory Authorities: End-User Statements and transaction records may be disclosed to Canadian or U.S. export control authorities upon legal request.
  • Service Providers: We use Formspree to securely route form submissions to our corporate inbox.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to request the deletion of your RFQ data, please contact us at sales@micasupply.ca.
